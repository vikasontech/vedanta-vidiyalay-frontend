Description

This software is intended to provide and open source solution for the school management ERP. This is just beginning and we would like to provide a great software solution for our school that helps us to grow.

PS: This site may be slow some time because it hosted on heroku free dyno that goes to sleep after some time.

Feature

  • Manage students records by their class/standard

  • Fees management

  • Fine management

Many more to come soon ...
